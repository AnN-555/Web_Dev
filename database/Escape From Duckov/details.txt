The Hardcore Extraction Shooter... with more Quack.

Welcome to Duckov, a city torn apart by feathered warfare and bread-crumb politics. In Escape From Duckov, you aren't just a soldier; you are a Mallard of Fortune. This is a high-stakes, tactical "Extraction Honker" where every waddle could be your last.

Will you escape with the legendary Golden Crust, or will you end up as Peking Duck?
