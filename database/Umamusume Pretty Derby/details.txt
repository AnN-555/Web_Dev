Run for Your Dreams!

In a world where the legends of great racehorses are reborn as "Horse Girls" (Umamusume) with extraordinary speed and spirit, your journey as a rookie Trainer begins. At the prestigious Tracen Academy, you will guide these talented girls through rigorous training, heartfelt moments, and the ultimate pursuit of the "Twinkle Series" championship.

Can you lead your Umamusume to the top of the podium and help them shine on the center stage?
